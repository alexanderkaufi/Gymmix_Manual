# Settings and Subscription

## Settings

Path: Main Menu -> `Settings`

Use settings to personalize Gymmix.

![Settings overview](../../assets/images/figures/settings-overview.png)

## Customize Design

Path: `Settings` -> `Select Theme`

Choose your preferred app color theme.

![Theme selection](../../assets/images/figures/settings-theme.png)

## Select Units

Path: `Settings` -> `Select Units`

Set global measurement units, for example:

- kg or lbs
- cm or in

![Units configuration](../../assets/images/figures/settings-units.png)

## Gymmix Subscription

Path: `Settings` -> `Manage subscriptions`

Manage your subscription and unlock pro features.

![Subscription management](../../assets/images/figures/settings-subscription.png)
