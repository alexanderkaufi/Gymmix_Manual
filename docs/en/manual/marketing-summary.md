# Gymmix Marketing Summary

Gymmix is your focused workout app for planning, tracking, and measurable progress.

This page is intentionally short and designed for first-time visitors who want a quick overview.

## What you can do with Gymmix

- Build workout plans in just a few steps
- Log exercises with weight and reps during each session
- Review progress with statistics and archived workouts
- Personalize design, units, and subscription settings

## Start in 60 seconds

1. Open `Training Overview`.
2. Create your first workout with `Add Training`.
3. Add exercises through `Add Exercise`.
4. Start your session and log sets in the active exercise view.

## Why Gymmix?

- Clear structure without unnecessary complexity
- Fast interaction during workouts
- Reliable history for long-term progress tracking

## Who is it for?

- Beginners who want a simple training flow
- Intermediate and advanced users who track performance consistently
- Anyone who wants workouts and progress data in one place
